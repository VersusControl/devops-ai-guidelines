## Prerequisites Checklist

### Required Knowledge

- [ ] **Basic DevOps**: Linux, Git, CI/CD concepts
- [ ] **Cloud Fundamentals**: At least one cloud provider (AWS/Azure/GCP)
- [ ] **Programming**: Python basics, scripting experience
- [ ] **Infrastructure**: Understanding of servers, networking, containers

- [ ] **Terraform/CloudFormation**: Infrastructure as Code
- [ ] **Kubernetes**: Container orchestration
- [ ] **Monitoring**: Prometheus, Grafana, or similar

## Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

### **Technical Pitfalls**

- ‚ùå **Over-reliance on AI**: Always understand what the AI generates
- ‚ùå **Ignoring Security**: Implement proper validation and safeguards
- ‚ùå **Vendor Lock-in**: Build abstraction layers and multi-provider strategies
- ‚ùå **Skipping Fundamentals**: Maintain strong infrastructure foundations

### **Career Pitfalls**

- ‚ùå **Technology Tunnel Vision**: Focus on principles, not just tools
- ‚ùå **Isolation**: Stay connected with both AI and DevOps communities
- ‚ùå **Perfectionism**: Ship working solutions, iterate based on feedback
- ‚ùå **Neglecting Soft Skills**: Develop communication and leadership abilities
